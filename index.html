Programme rules
Soviet hybrid. Climb to a top set, drop back for volume. Best of both worlds for strength and fat loss simultaneously.
Deload weeks 4, 8, 12. Eat at maintenance. Non-negotiable — this is when adaptation happens.
Protein every day. 170–200g daily. Missing this is the main reason strength stalls in a deficit.
Sleep 7–9 hours. Under 7 raises cortisol and ghrelin — both stall fat loss even in a proper deficit.
3.5L water daily. At 127kg training 6 days a week, hydration directly impacts both performance and recovery.
Nothing above 85% on SBD day. One day rest before 1RM testing. Confidence, not a PR.
